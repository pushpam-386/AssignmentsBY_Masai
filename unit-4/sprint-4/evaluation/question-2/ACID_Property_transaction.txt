Q.2. What is the ACID acronym for? 
     Describe each property individually with your own words

Answer-->To maintain consistency in a database during the transaction, there 
are certain properties known as ACID property.

	ACID means=Atomicity
		  =Consistency
		  =Isolation
		  =Durability
		  

Atomicity->This means transaction does'nt occur partially, 
either it will happen completly else it will not occur at all.
        It is also known as "All or Nothing property".

Consistency->To maintain consistency of DataBase before and after 
the transaction.

Isolation->To ensure that transaction don't occur simultansly and 
transaction occurs independently without any interfarence.

Durability->After the completion transaction this update 
and modify the database.
