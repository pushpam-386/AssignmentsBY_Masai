1. Upper bound Wildcards:-

These wildcards can be used when you want to relax the restrictions on a variable.
Example= public static void add(List<? extends Number> List)


2. Lower bounded Wildcards:-
 
It is expressed using the wildcards character('?'), followed by the super keywords,
followed by its lower bound.

Syntax: Collectiontype<? super A>


-->>we use extendred key word when we want to get values out of a structure and super
    wildcards when you put values in structure.
